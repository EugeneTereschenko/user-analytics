Getting Started

To begin, sign up for an account and complete your profile. Use the navigation bar to access dashboards and reports.

Managing Your Profile

You can edit your profile details, experience, and skills from the Profile section.

Analytics Dashboard

The dashboard shows analytics such as user signups, active users, and device usage.

FAQ

  • How do I reset my password? Go to the Sign In page and click "Forgot Password".
  • How do I invite new users? Navigate to Users and click "Invite User".
  • How do I generate reports? Visit the Reports page to create new reports.
